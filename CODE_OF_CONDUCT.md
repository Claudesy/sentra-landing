# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a
respectful and professional experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Our Standards

**Expected behaviour:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the project and community

**Unacceptable behaviour:**
- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments in any form
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Conduct that could reasonably be considered unprofessional

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the project maintainers at
**conduct@claudesy.com**. All reports will be reviewed confidentially and responded to
promptly. Maintainers who do not follow this Code of Conduct may face consequences
as determined by the project leadership.
